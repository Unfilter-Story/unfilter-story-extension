Primary Sourcing Protocols

We require on-the-record, primary verification for all technical and financial claims. Secondary aggregation is strictly labeled. Anonymous sources are only permitted when corroborated by encrypted documentary evidence.

02

Capital & Equity Disclosures

Any editorial staff holding equity, tokens, or advisory roles in a covered entity must declare it in the article metadata. Coverage of direct competitors to investments is restricted.
